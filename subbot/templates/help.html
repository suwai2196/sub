Welcome

SubBot framework is a tool for primary students and teachers which will help in the provision of learning support. Across the framework, the teachers will acquire the ability to develop the knowledge of the chatbot, and the resulted chatbot will provide answers to the students.

Question Types

The chatbot is able to reply to 7 different question types.

  • Definition Question
    Example:
    What is an acute angle?
  • Example Question
    Example:
    What is an example of acute angle?
  • Objective Question
    Example:
    Why am I studying about angle?
  • Difference Question
    Example:
    What is the difference between acute angle and right angle?
  • Procedure Question
    Example:
    I don't know how to do angle addition postulate.
  • Feature Specification Question
    Example:
    What are the components of an angle?
  • Confirmation Question
    Example:
    1. Is an acute angle an angle less than 90 degrees?
    2. Is it true that acute angle a type of an angle?
    3. Is a vertice one of the component of the angle?
